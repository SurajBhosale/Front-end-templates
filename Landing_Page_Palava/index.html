Safety and Security

×

Palava has invested heavily in technology and world class processes to deal with safety for its citizens and businesses to grow and flourish in the city.

- A dedicated 24X7, 911 emergency number has been setup to deal with emergency situations such as theft, fire, and medical services.
- Emergency response team can reach any place within Palava in 10 min.
- Over 700 CCTVs are used for video surveillance and to monitor the public places and common areas in the city
- Common areas such as clubhouses and buildings have strict access controls for enhanced security

Transport Management

×

Palava is strategically located at the centre of Navi Mumbai, Thane and Kalyan and is well connected through bus and rail services.

- Palava has over 30 dedicated buses that travel within Palava and to key business hubs.
- Shuttle services provide easy connectivity to business hubs like Powai, Andheri, BKC, Lower Parel and Vashi.
- Dedicated AC buses shuttle citizens to the nearby stations of Dombivli and Ghansoli
- These buses offer online booking facilities, app based tracking and ply at a frequency of 30 min.
- Palava is well connected through rail network to Dombivli (Central), Vashi (Harbour) & Vasai (Western)
- Closest station, Nilje has 16 trains that connects Diva, Panvel & Vasai
- Palava has tied up with TILT that offers environment friendly mobility solution through fitness & electric cycles.

Waste Management

×

Palava aims to create a smart waste management system which views waste as a resource and ensures that less than 5% waste goes to the landfill.

- Separate bins are provided in the city for recyclable and non-recyclable wastes.
- A net zero solid waste management plant converts the waste into manure for landscaping and bio-gas for generating electricity for the plant.
